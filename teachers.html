Teacher - Nhessica

Nhessica is a Melbourne-based Voice Teacher modelling the Estill Voice Training to develop vocal skills and all aspects of voice. Nhessica was awarded the Advanced Diploma of Music Industry, and Certificate IV in Training and Assessment from Melbourne Polytechnic.

Music, dance and acting are her biggest passions that she takes pride in. Her love towards music expanded to not only performing on stage, but also having a heart to teach and train others, specifically to use their voice in an innovative way. Nhessica also has the ability to teach in different genres such as pop music, rock, contemporary, musical theatre, latin, gospel and world music.

After completing her studies in music, she was in a professional dancing scene for both commercial and corporate events, acted in an international movie and several music videos in Melbourne. Nhessica secured her first voice teaching job at Melbourne Voiceworks last year, and currently teaches at A Major Music Academy, whilst attending a focus group to expand her professional development.

Credentials

Anita completed her Bachelor of Music majoring in Jazz and Contemporary Performance at Monash University and has completed her Estill Voice Training Level One and Level Two studies. Anita keeps up to date with the latest techniques in vocal training. She has participated in workshops with local and international artists both in Brazil and Melbourne which has given her rich and varied networks.

Approach

Anita sees the potential in every student. She works with her students to see them develop new skills and grow in confidence. Fun is an important component of the lesson enabling them to deepen their love and appreciation of music and the voice. Anita helps students realise that they already have endless possibilities and potential in regard to their voice that can be developed and strengthened. She helps students to have a growth mindset allowing them to soar to new heights and to explore new sounds working with them in a systematic way to cater to individual learning styles.

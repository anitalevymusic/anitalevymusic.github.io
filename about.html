Welcome To A Major Music Academy!


We are a young company, with a big heart. Bringing enthusiasm into our lessons, we hope to empart the same creative joy that we ourselves find by composing and developing our skills musically. Starting with singing, as our foundation, we offer 1 on 1 class-time. In special circumstances, we have offered an online option for our students.

We have big dreams too, and are excited for what the future has in store for our little team. Stay tuned for a range of instrumental lessons to follow!

Our Mission

To empower students by equipping them with tools to develop their voice and musical expression.

We Value

Confidence & Resilience
Vocal Health & Voice Development
Versatility & Musicality
Imagination & Creativity
